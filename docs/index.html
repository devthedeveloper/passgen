Why passgen
EVERYTHING
YOU NEED.
üîê
Cryptographically secure

Uses Go's crypto/rand backed by the OS CSPRNG. No weak math/rand seeding. Every character is genuinely unpredictable.

‚ö°
Quick mode

passgen - or passgen _ ‚Äî one keystroke, instant segmented password. Copied to clipboard before you blink.

üí¨
Interactive mode

Run passgen with no flags and it walks you through every option step by step. No manual needed.

üß©
Segmented passwords

Generate readable patterns like ab12-cd34-ef56. Configure number of segments, chars per segment, and separator.

üß†
Passphrases

Easy to remember, hard to crack. Use your own words with -include, scramble letters with -shuffle-chars, or go fully random from 800+ words.

üìã
Auto clipboard

Every generated password is instantly copied to your clipboard. Supports pbcopy, xclip, xsel, wl-copy, and clip.

üåç
Cross-platform binary

Single static binary. macOS (Apple Silicon + Intel), Linux (amd64 + arm64), Windows. Drop in PATH and go.

  1  Random

Random passwords
passgen -length 32
passgen -count 5
passgen -no-symbols
passgen -exclude "0OIl1"
passgen -length 24 -count 3
Segmented passwords
passgen -type segment
passgen -type segment -segments 4
passgen -type segment -seg-length 6
passgen -type segment -separator _
Passphrases (easy to remember)
passgen -type phrase
Tiger-Maple-Cloud-Sage-97

passgen -type phrase -words 5
passgen -type phrase -separator _
passgen -type phrase -capitalize=false
Passphrases with your words
passgen -type phrase -include "dhruv is great"
Dhruv-Is-Great-853

passgen -type phrase -include "sun moon" -words 4
Sun-Moon-Cage-Bolt-217

passgen -type phrase -include "dhruv is great" -shuffle-chars
Ruvhd-Si-Gerta-78
